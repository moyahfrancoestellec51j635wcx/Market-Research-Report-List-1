<p><p>Rubber conveyor belts are classified into three main types: multiply fabric conveyor belts, steel cord conveyor belts, and solid woven conveyor belts. Multiply fabric conveyor belts are composed of layers of fabric materials for flexibility and strength. Steel cord conveyor belts have steel cords for high tensile strength and durability. Solid woven conveyor belts use solid woven fabric for excellent impact resistance. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and applications, making them essential components in various industries worldwide.</p></p>
