<p><p>PF Phenol-methanal, also known as Phenol-Formaldehyde, is used in two main market types: Novolac and Resoles. Novolac resins are thermoplastic and require a curing agent to cross-link, making them ideal for applications like coatings and adhesives. Resoles, on the other hand, are thermosetting resins that cure under heat, commonly used in products like plywood and electrical insulation. Both types of PF resins offer a range of properties and applications, making them versatile materials in various industries.</p></p>
