<p><strong>The Conjugate Vaccines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monovalent Conjugate Vaccines</li><li>Multivalent Conjugate Vaccines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conjugate vaccines are classified into two types: monovalent and multivalent. Monovalent conjugate vaccines target a single strain or serotype of bacteria, while multivalent conjugate vaccines target multiple strains or serotypes. Monovalent vaccines are typically used to prevent diseases caused by a single strain, such as Haemophilus influenzae type b. Multivalent vaccines, on the other hand, can provide protection against multiple strains of bacteria, like pneumococcal conjugate vaccines which target several strains of Streptococcus pneumoniae.</p></p>
