Horizontal furnaces offer better uniformity and control over the annealing process, making them suitable for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. The "Others" category includes specialized furnaces that cater to specific requirements such as batch processing or ultra-high-temperature annealing. Each type of furnace offers unique advantages depending on the specific needs of semiconductor manufacturers.</p></p>
