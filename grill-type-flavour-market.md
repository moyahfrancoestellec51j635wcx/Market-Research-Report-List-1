<p><p>Grill-type flavor market consists of two main types: powder and liquid. Powder grill flavors are dry blends of seasonings and herbs that can be easily sprinkled onto food before grilling. They are convenient for storage and have a longer shelf life. Liquid grill flavors, on the other hand, are concentrated liquid extracts that can be brushed or marinated onto food before cooking. They provide a more intense and immediate flavor. Both powder and liquid grill flavors cater to different consumer preferences and cooking methods.</p></p>
