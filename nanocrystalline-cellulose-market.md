<p><p>Nanocrystalline Cellulose Market includes three main types: Nanofibrillated Cellulose (NFC), Nanocrystalline Cellulose (NCC), and Bacterial Nanocellulose. NFC is a type of cellulose consisting of nanoscale diameter fibrils, NCC is composed of highly crystalline cellulose nanoparticles, and Bacterial Nanocellulose is produced by bacteria through fermentation. These materials have various applications in industries such as composites, biomedical, coatings, and food packaging due to their unique properties like high strength, biodegradability, and renewable sourcing.</p></p>
