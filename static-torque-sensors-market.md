<p><p>Static torque sensors are devices used to measure the amount of torque or rotational force applied to an object. Two main types of static torque sensors are rotary torque sensors and reaction torque sensors. Rotary torque sensors measure the torque applied to a rotating shaft, while reaction torque sensors measure the torque applied to a non-rotating object. Both types of sensors are essential in a variety of industries for measuring and monitoring torque levels in machinery and equipment.</p></p>
