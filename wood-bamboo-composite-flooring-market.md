<p><p>Wood-bamboo composite flooring comes in various types to suit different preferences and requirements. Solid floor is made entirely of wood-bamboo composite material, offering traditional aesthetics and durability. Engineered floor consists of a layer of wood-bamboo composite on top of plywood or high-density fiberboard, providing stability and moisture resistance. Strand woven floor uses compressed bamboo strands for a unique and highly durable finish. Other markets may include hybrid composite flooring options or specialized finishes for specific purposes, catering to a wide range of consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
