<p><p>Fermented food and ingredients are foods that have undergone the process of fermentation, in which beneficial bacteria or yeast break down sugars and starches to create lactic acid. Factors such as the rising demand for natural and organic products, as well as the growing popularity of ethnic cuisines and flavors, are contributing to the market's expansion. In addition, advancements in fermentation technology and processes are enabling manufacturers to introduce innovative and unique fermented products to cater to consumer preferences.</p><p>According to market research, the Fermented Food and Ingredients Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.70% during the forecast period. Consumers are attracted to fermented foods due to their potential health benefits, including improved digestive health, boosted immunity, and enhanced flavor profiles. As the trend towards healthy and functional foods continues to rise, the fermented food and ingredients market is poised for further growth in the coming years.</p></p>
