<p><p>Caliper Integrated EPB refers to an electronic parking brake system where the functionality is integrated within the caliper assembly. Both types of EPB systems are gaining traction in the automotive industry due to their efficiency, safety, and ease of integration with modern vehicle technologies.</p></p>
