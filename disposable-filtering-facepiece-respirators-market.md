<p><p>Disposable Filtering Facepiece Respirators come in two main types - valved and unvalved. Valved respirators have a one-way valve that allows for easier exhalation, reducing heat and moisture buildup inside the mask. They provide more comfort for the wearer but may not offer as much protection for others as unvalved respirators. Unvalved respirators do not have a valve, offering more protection for others as they filter both the air being inhaled and exhaled. Both types are commonly used in industrial settings, healthcare facilities, and by the general public during disease outbreaks.</p></p>
