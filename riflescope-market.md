<p><ul><li>Telescopic Sight</li><li>Collimating Optical Sight</li><li>Reflex Sight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Riflescope market encompasses various types of sights, including Telescopic Sight, Collimating Optical Sight, and Reflex Sight. Telescopic sights utilize magnifying lenses to provide a clear view of distant targets. Collimating optical sights offer a precise aiming point by projecting a holographic reticle onto the target. Reflect sights, on the other hand, use a reflective glass to superimpose an illuminated red dot onto the target for quick aiming. Each type of sight caters to different shooting preferences and situations, catering to the diverse needs of riflescope users.</p></p>
