<p><p>Automotive engine coolants are available in various types such as ethylene glycol, propylene glycol, and glycerin. Ethylene glycol is the most commonly used coolant due to its excellent heat transfer properties and anti-freezing capabilities. Propylene glycol is a safer alternative to ethylene glycol, as it is less toxic. Glycerin is a natural coolant derived from plant oils and is also non-toxic. Each type of coolant has its own advantages and is used depending on the specific requirements of the vehicle.</p></p>
