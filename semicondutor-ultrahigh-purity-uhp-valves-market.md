<p><p>Semiconductor Ultrahigh Purity (UHP) Valves come in two main types: Low Flow and High Flow. Low Flow valves are designed for applications that require precise control of small amounts of gas or liquid, making them ideal for tasks such as gas chromatography or dosing systems. On the other hand, High Flow valves are used in applications that require the rapid transfer of large volumes of gas or liquid, such as in semiconductor manufacturing or vacuum systems. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the purity and efficiency of processes in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
