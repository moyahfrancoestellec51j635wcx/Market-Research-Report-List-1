<p><p>The rail wheel and axle market consists of two main types: rolled wheels & axles for railways and forged wheels & axles for railways. Rolled wheels & axles are made by shaping heated metal through rollers, resulting in a cost-effective and widely used option. On the other hand, forged wheels & axles are made by heating metal and then shaping it through compressive force, resulting in a more durable and higher quality product. Both types cater to the railway industry's needs for reliable and efficient transportation solutions.</p></p>
