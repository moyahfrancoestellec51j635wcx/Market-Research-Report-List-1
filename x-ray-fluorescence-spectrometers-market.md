<p><strong>The X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Energy Dispersive Type</li><li>Wavelength Dispersive Type</li><li>Polarized Energy Dispersive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometers are analytical instruments used to determine the elemental composition of various materials. There are three main types of X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometers: Energy Dispersive Type, Wavelength Dispersive Type, and Polarized Energy Dispersive Type. </p><p>- Energy Dispersive Type spectrometers use a semiconductor detector to measure the energy of X-rays emitted from the sample.</p><p>- Wavelength Dispersive Type spectrometers utilize diffraction crystals to separate X-rays based on their wavelengths.</p><p>- Polarized Energy Dispersive Type spectrometers combine the energy-dispersive technique with a polarization device to enhance detection sensitivity.</p></p>
