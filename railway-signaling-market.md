<p><p>Railway signaling market is segmented into Communication Based Train Control (CBTC), Positive Train Control (PTC), and Automatic Train Control (ATC). CBTC systems use communication technology to control train movements, ensuring safe and efficient operations. PTC systems prevent train collisions and employ GPS and communication systems to monitor train locations. ATC systems regulate train speeds and distances between trains to enhance safety and efficiency. These technologies collectively improve railway operations by enhancing communication, monitoring, and control of train movements.</p></p>
