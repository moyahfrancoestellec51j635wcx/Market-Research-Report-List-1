<p><p>Hard coal market types typically include Lump Anthracite and Anthracite Fines. Lump Anthracite refers to larger pieces of anthracite coal, while Anthracite Fines are smaller pieces or particles of the coal. Lump Anthracite is often preferred for its longer burning time and lower ash content, making it suitable for some heating applications. Anthracite Fines, on the other hand, are commonly used in industrial processes or for blending with other coals to improve combustion efficiency. Both types play important roles in the hard coal market.</p></p>
