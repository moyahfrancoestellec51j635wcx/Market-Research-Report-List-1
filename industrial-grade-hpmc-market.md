<p><p>Industrial Grade HPMC is categorized into three types based on their viscosity levels: Low Viscosity, Middle Viscosity, and High Viscosity. Low Viscosity HPMC is commonly used in industries where fast dissolution and dispersion are required. Middle Viscosity HPMC finds applications in industries that require a balance between flow properties and retention. High Viscosity HPMC is preferred in industries where improved water retention and thickening properties are necessary. Each type of HPMC caters to specific industrial needs based on their viscosity characteristics.</p></p>
