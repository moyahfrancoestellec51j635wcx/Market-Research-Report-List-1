<p><p>Attitude and Heading Reference Systems (AHRS) are commonly categorized into three types based on their functionality: Conventional AHRS, Air Data AHRS, and GPS-aided AHRS. Conventional AHRS rely on internal sensors to determine aircraft attitude and heading. Air Data AHRS incorporate air data sensors in addition to internal sensors for improved accuracy. GPS-aided AHRS combine GPS data with internal sensors to provide precise positioning information. Each type caters to specific aircraft requirements and operational needs in the aviation industry.</p></p>
