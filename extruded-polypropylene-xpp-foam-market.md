<p><p>Extruded Polypropylene (XPP) Foam is categorized into two types based on density - Low-Density XPP Foam and High-Density XPP Foam. Low-Density XPP Foam is characterized by its lightweight and soft texture, making it ideal for cushioning and packaging applications. On the other hand, High-Density XPP Foam is more rigid and durable, suitable for applications requiring higher strength and impact resistance. Both types of XPP foam offer excellent thermal insulation properties, chemical resistance, and recyclability, making them versatile materials in various industries.</p></p>
