<p><strong>The Liquild Non-woven Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Meltblown</li><li>Needle Felt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid non-woven filter market consists of two main types: meltblown and needle felt. Meltblown filters are made by blowing hot air through a fiber matrix to create a fine mesh, ideal for capturing small particles. Needle felt filters, on the other hand, are created by interlocking fibers using barbed needles, offering a higher level of filtration efficiency. Both types are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, healthcare, and industrial applications to remove contaminants from liquids.</p></p>
