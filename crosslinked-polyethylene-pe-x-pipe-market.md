<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Crosslinked Polyethylene (PE-X) Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE-Xa</li><li>PE-Xb</li><li>PE-Xc</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crosslinked Polyethylene (PE-X) pipes are classified into three main types - PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc based on the method of crosslinking used in their production. PE-Xa pipes are crosslinked using the peroxide method, which results in excellent high-temperature resistance and flexibility. PE-Xb pipes are crosslinked through the silane method, offering good temperature and pressure resistance. PE-Xc pipes are crosslinked using the electron beam method, providing enhanced resistance to oxidation and chlorine. Each type has unique characteristics suited for different applications.</p></p>
