<p><p>Oocyte retrieval needles are commonly categorized into two types: Single Lumen and Double Lumen. Single Lumen needles have a single channel for extracting oocytes during the retrieval process. On the other hand, Double Lumen needles have two separate channels - one for aspiration and one for flushing. Single Lumen needles are typically used for simpler procedures, while Double Lumen needles are preferred for more complex cases where additional flushing is required for better oocyte retrieval results.</p></p>
