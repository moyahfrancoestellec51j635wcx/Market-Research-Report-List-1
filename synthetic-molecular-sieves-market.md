The market for synthetic molecular sieves includes Zeolite A, Type X, Type Y, Ultra-stable Y (USY), and ZSM-5. Zeolite A is commonly used as a sodium selective sieve, while Type X and Type Y are used in petrochemical and gas separation applications. Ultra-stable Y (USY) offers improved durability, while ZSM-5 is known for its acid catalytic properties, used in the production of fuels and chemicals.</p></p>
