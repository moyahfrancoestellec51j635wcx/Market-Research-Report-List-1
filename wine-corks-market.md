<p><ul><li>Natural Corks</li><li>Synthetic Corks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural corks are made from the bark of cork oak trees and are the traditional choice for sealing wine bottles. They are preferred by many winemakers for their ability to allow small amounts of oxygen to enter the bottle, allowing the wine to age gracefully. Synthetic corks are made from materials like plastic or plant-based polymers and are a more affordable and consistent option. They are popular for everyday wines and are also considered more environmentally friendly than natural corks.</p></p>
