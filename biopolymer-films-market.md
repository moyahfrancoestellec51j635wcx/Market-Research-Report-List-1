<p><ul><li>Bio-based</li><li>Microbial Synthesized</li><li>Synthetic</li><li>Partially Bio-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biopolymer films can be classified into different types based on their source and synthesis method. Bio-based films are derived from renewable resources such as plants, animals, and microorganisms. Microbial-synthesized films are produced by bacteria or fungi through fermentation processes. Synthetic films are derived from non-renewable resources such as petroleum. Partially bio-based films are a combination of bio-based and synthetic materials. Each type of biopolymer film offers unique advantages in terms of sustainability, biodegradability, and performance.</p></p>
