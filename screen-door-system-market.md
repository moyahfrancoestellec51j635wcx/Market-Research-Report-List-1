<p><p>Screen door systems are categorized into two main types based on their height: full height and half height. Full height screen door systems extend from the floor to the ceiling, providing complete coverage and privacy. They are commonly used in offices and reception areas. Half-height screen door systems are shorter and typically extend to about waist or chest level, allowing for a more open and airy feel. They are often found in collaborative workspaces and meeting rooms. Both types offer a modern solution for creating separate spaces within a larger area.</p></p>
