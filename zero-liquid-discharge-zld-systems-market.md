<p><p>Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) systems are designed to eliminate liquid waste discharge by treating and recovering all wastewater. There are two main types of ZLD systems: Conventional and Hybrid. Conventional ZLD systems utilize a combination of technologies such as evaporation, crystallization, and membrane processes to achieve complete water recovery. On the other hand, Hybrid ZLD systems combine conventional technologies with newer innovations like forward osmosis and electrocoagulation to enhance performance and efficiency. Both types of ZLD systems play a crucial role in reducing environmental impact and conserving water resources.</p></p>
