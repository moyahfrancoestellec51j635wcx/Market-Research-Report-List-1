Conventional solvents are derived from petroleum and are widely used in various industries. Bio solvents are made from renewable sources like corn or soybeans, offering a more sustainable alternative. Green solvents are made from bio-based materials and are environmentally friendly. These solvent types are gaining popularity due to their reduced environmental impact and health benefits compared to traditional solvent options.</p></p>
