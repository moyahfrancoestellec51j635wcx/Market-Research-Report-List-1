<p><strong>The UV Cured Printing Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Arc Curing</li><li>LED Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV cured printing inks market is dominated by two types of curing methods - Arc Curing and LED Curing. Arc Curing involves the use of high-intensity UV lamps that emit UV light to cure the ink quickly, while LED Curing uses light-emitting diodes to generate UV light for curing. Arc curing is more traditional and widely used due to its higher intensity and faster curing times, while LED curing is gaining popularity for its energy efficiency and longer lifespan. Both methods offer fast, high-quality curing for UV printing inks.</p></p>
