<p><p>Hybrid drivetrain market types consist of Plug-in Hybrid Electric (PHE), Full Hybrid Electric (HE), and Mild Hybrid Electric (HE). PHE vehicles can run on both electricity and gasoline, offering extended electric range. Full HE vehicles can run on electric power alone at low speeds and switch to gasoline at higher speeds. Mild HE vehicles have a smaller electric motor and battery, assisting the gasoline engine to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions. Each type offers unique benefits for consumers seeking more efficient and environmentally-friendly transportation options.</p></p>
