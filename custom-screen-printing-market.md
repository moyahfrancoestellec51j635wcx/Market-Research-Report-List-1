<p><p>Custom screen printing is a technique of printing images, designs, or logos onto various surfaces, such as textiles, clothing, ceramics, metal, glass, and paper. Businesses and individuals alike are increasingly seeking unique designs and personalized products to stand out from the crowd. Improved printing techniques, such as water-based and discharge printing, have also contributed to the market's expansion, enabling the production of softer and more detailed prints on a variety of materials.</p><p>Overall, the custom screen printing market is poised for substantial growth, driven by increasing customization demands, technological advancements, and the expanding reach of online platforms.</p></p>
