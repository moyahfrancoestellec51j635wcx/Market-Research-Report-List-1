<p><p>Pressure Compensated Dropper Tape is a type of irrigation system used in both surface drip and subsurface drip irrigation markets. In surface drip irrigation, the tape is laid on the soil surface and delivers water directly to the root zone of plants. In subsurface drip irrigation, the tape is buried beneath the soil surface to provide water directly to the roots. This type of irrigation system is designed to maintain a consistent flow rate regardless of variations in pressure, ensuring uniform water distribution across the field.</p></p>
