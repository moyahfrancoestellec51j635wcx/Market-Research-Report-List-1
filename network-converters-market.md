<p><p>Network converters are devices used to connect different types of network cables, enabling smooth communication between various systems. The market for network converters can be divided into three types: Copper to Fiber, Fiber to Fiber, and Copper to Copper. Copper to Fiber converters allow the conversion of Ethernet signals from copper cables to fiber-optic cables, facilitating long-distance data transmission. Fiber to Fiber converters enable the connection between two fiber-optic cables, enabling seamless communication between different fiber networks. Copper to Copper converters convert Ethernet signals from one copper cable to another, enabling compatibility between devices using copper connections.</p></p>
