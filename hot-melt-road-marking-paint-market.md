<p><p>Hot melt road marking paint is available in different types such as water-based and solvent-based. Water-based paints use water as the main solvent, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. They offer good durability and are suitable for indoor and outdoor applications. On the other hand, solvent-based paints contain organic solvents that evaporate during the drying process. These paints provide excellent adhesion to surfaces and are more resistant to wear and tear. Both types of paints are commonly used for road marking applications.</p></p>
