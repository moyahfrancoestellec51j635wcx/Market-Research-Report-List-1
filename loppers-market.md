<p><p>Loppers are available in two main types - anvil type and bypass type. Anvil loppers have a single blade that cuts against a flat edge, making them more suitable for cutting dead or thick branches. On the other hand, bypass loppers have two blades that pass by each other, providing a cleaner and more precise cut for live branches. Both types have their own advantages and are preferred by different users based on their cutting needs and preferences.</p></p>
