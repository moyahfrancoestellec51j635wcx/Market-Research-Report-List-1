<p><p>Specialty contact lenses are categorized into three main types based on their materials and designs: Rigid contact lenses, Hybrid contact lenses, and Soft contact lenses. Rigid contact lenses provide clear vision for those with severe refractive errors. Hybrid lenses combine the benefits of both rigid and soft lenses for improved comfort and visual acuity. Soft contact lenses are comfortable to wear for extended periods and come in various options such as daily disposables and colored lenses. Each type serves a unique purpose in addressing different vision needs.</p></p>
