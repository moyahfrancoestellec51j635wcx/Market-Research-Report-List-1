<p><p>The Doorbell market consists of three main types: Wired Doorbells, Wireless Visible Doorbells, and Wireless Invisible Doorbells. Wired Doorbells require a physical connection to the home's electrical system for operation. Wireless Visible Doorbells do not require any wiring and feature a camera to provide visual communication with visitors. Wireless Invisible Doorbells also do not require wiring but do not have a camera, instead using sensors to detect when someone is at the door. Each type offers different features to suit the needs of consumers.</p></p>
