<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Condo insurance market types, also known as Type I and Type II, categorize condominium insurance policies based on the extent of coverage they provide. Type I policies typically offer coverage for the structure of the condo unit, while Type II policies include coverage for both the structure and personal belongings within the unit. Type II policies are usually more comprehensive and may also cover additional living expenses in case the unit becomes uninhabitable. The choice between Type I and Type II policies depends on the level of protection desired by the policyholder.</p></p>
