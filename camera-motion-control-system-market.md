<p><ul><li>2-axis Type</li><li>3-axis Type</li><li>4-axis Type</li><li>5-axis Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Camera motion control systems come in various types based on the number of axes they control. 2-axis types control movement on two planes, typically pan and tilt. 3-axis types add a third dimension, usually roll, for more dynamic shots. 4-axis types introduce a fourth axis, like focus or zoom control. 5-axis types provide even more flexibility with extra axes for advanced camera movements. Other types may include specialized systems with unique functionalities tailored to specific filming needs. Each type offers different levels of control and creativity for filmmakers.</p></p>
