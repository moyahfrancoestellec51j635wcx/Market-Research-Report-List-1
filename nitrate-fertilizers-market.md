<p><p>Nitrate fertilizers are commonly used in agriculture to provide essential nitrogen to plants. Three main types of nitrate fertilizers on the market are Calcium Ammonium Nitrate (CAN), Ammonium Nitrate (AN), and Calcium Nitrate (CN). CAN is a combination of calcium and ammonium nitrate, providing both quick and long-lasting sources of nitrogen. AN is a highly soluble form of nitrogen that is absorbed quickly by plants. CN is a water-soluble fertilizer that provides a source of calcium along with nitrogen for plant growth.</p></p>
