<p><p>Potassium fertilizer market types include Muriate of Potash (MOP), Sulfate of Potash (SOP), and others. MOP is the most common type, containing higher potassium levels but also chloride which may negatively impact crops in some situations. SOP, on the other hand, is chloride-free and more suitable for chloride-sensitive crops. Other types of potassium fertilizers may include blends or specialty formulations to meet specific crop needs or environmental considerations. Each type offers different benefits and considerations for agricultural use.</p></p>
