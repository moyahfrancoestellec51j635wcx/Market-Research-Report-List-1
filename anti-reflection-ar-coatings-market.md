<p><p>Anti-Reflection (AR) coatings are commonly classified into various types based on the technology used for their deposition. Vacuum deposition involves the use of a vacuum chamber to deposit thin films on a substrate. E-beam evaporation utilizes an electron beam to evaporate materials and create coatings. Sputtering involves bombarding a target material with high-energy particles to deposit a coating. Other types of AR coatings may include plasma-enhanced chemical vapor deposition (PECVD) or dip coating methods. Each method has its own advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
