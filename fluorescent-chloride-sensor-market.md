<p><ul><li>Quinoline-Based Chloride</li><li>Forster Resonance Energy Transfer Based Chloride</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorescent chloride sensors are used for detecting chloride ions in various applications. Quinoline-based chloride sensors function by binding to chloride ions, causing a change in their fluorescent properties. Forster Resonance Energy Transfer (FRET) based chloride sensors work by transferring energy from one dye molecule to another when chloride ions are present, resulting in a measurable fluorescence signal. Both types of sensors are highly sensitive and selective in detecting chloride ions, making them valuable tools in analytical chemistry and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
