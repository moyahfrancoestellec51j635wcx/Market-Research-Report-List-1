<p><p>Bridge Machine, Horizontal Machine, and Articulated-Arm Machines are three common types of Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM). Bridge Machines are characterized by their fixed bridge structure, making them suitable for measuring large and heavy parts. Horizontal Machines are ideal for measuring thin and flat components due to their horizontal orientation. Articulated-Arm Machines use a flexible arm with multiple joints to reach intricate areas for measurement. Other types of CMM include portable and gantry machines, each catering to specific measurement needs in various industries.</p></p>
