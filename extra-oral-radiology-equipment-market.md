<p><p>Extra oral radiology equipment market includes two main types - Panoramic X-ray Unit and Cephalometric X-ray Unit. Panoramic X-ray Units capture a full view of the teeth, jaws, and surrounding structures in a single image, making them ideal for routine dental examinations and treatment planning. Cephalometric X-ray Units focus on capturing side-view images of the head and skull, providing valuable information for orthodontic treatment planning and assessing growth patterns. Both types of equipment play crucial roles in modern dentistry, aiding in diagnosis and treatment decisions.</p></p>
