Fire-rated particle board is designed to withstand high temperatures and is suitable for use in furniture in areas where fire safety is a concern. Moisture-resistant particle board is treated to prevent water damage, making it ideal for use in bathrooms or kitchens. Standard particle board is the most commonly used type and is suitable for a wide range of furniture applications.</p></p>
