<p><p>Soda Photomask market comprises two main types - Soda Chrome Mask and Soda Emulsion Mask. Soda Chrome Mask is a type of photomask used in the semiconductor industry for patterning processes, where chromium is deposited on a glass plate to form the pattern. On the other hand, Soda Emulsion Mask uses a light-sensitive emulsion layer on a glass plate to create the pattern. Both types are essential in the manufacturing of integrated circuits and other semiconductor devices, catering to different needs and requirements in the industry.</p></p>
