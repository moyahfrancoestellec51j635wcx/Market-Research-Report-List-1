<p><p>Automatic bottle filling machines come in two main market types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic machines require minimal human intervention, as they can perform all tasks of bottle filling, capping, and labeling independently. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of manual intervention, such as loading bottles or controlling the machine's operations. Both types of machines offer various benefits depending on the production scale, efficiency, and labor costs of the manufacturing facility.</p></p>
