<p><strong>The Laptop Cooling Stand Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Cooling Stand (With Fan)</li><li>Passive Cooling Stand</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laptop cooling stands come in two main types: active and passive cooling stands. Active cooling stands typically include fans that help reduce the laptop's internal temperature by increasing airflow. They are powered by USB connections or batteries. On the other hand, passive cooling stands are designed to elevate the laptop, allowing for better airflow and heat dissipation without the use of fans. Passive cooling stands are often lightweight and portable, making them a popular choice for those who value simplicity and convenience.</p></p>
