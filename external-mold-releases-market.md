<p><p>Solvent-based External Mold Releases are made with solvents to create a barrier between the mold and the material being molded, making it easier to release the final product. On the other hand, Solvent-free External Mold Releases are made without solvents, using alternative methods to create the release barrier. Solvent-based releases are effective for a wide range of materials but may have environmental concerns, while solvent-free releases are eco-friendly and safer to use. Both types cater to different needs in the market, offering options for various applications.</p></p>
