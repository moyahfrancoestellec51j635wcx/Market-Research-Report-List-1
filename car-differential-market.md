<p><ul><li>Open Differential</li><li>Limited-Slip Differential</li><li>Locking Differential</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car differential market types include open differential, limited-slip differential, and locking differential. Open differentials allow wheels to rotate at different speeds, ideal for normal driving conditions. Limited-slip differentials provide some resistance to slippage, improving traction in varying road conditions. Locking differentials offer maximum traction by locking both wheels' rotation together, suitable for off-road or high-performance vehicles. Each type caters to different driving needs and preferences, providing users with options to enhance their driving experience in various environments.</p></p>
