<p><p>Elastic adhesives are widely used in various industries for their flexibility and durability. The market for elastic adhesives can be categorized into different types such as polyurethane, silicone, silane modified polymers (SMP), and others. Polyurethane adhesives offer high strength and flexibility, while silicone adhesives are known for their heat resistance and weatherability. SMP adhesives combine the best properties of both polyurethane and silicone adhesives.
