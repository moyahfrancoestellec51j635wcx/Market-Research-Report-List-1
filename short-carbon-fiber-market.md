<p><ul><li>PAN-Based Carbon Fiber</li><li>Pitch – Based Carbon Fiber</li><li>Rayon – Based Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Short carbon fiber market types include PAN-Based Carbon Fiber, Pitch-Based Carbon Fiber, and Rayon-Based Carbon Fiber. PAN-Based Carbon Fiber is made from polyacrylonitrile and is the most common type due to its high strength and stiffness. Pitch-Based Carbon Fiber is derived from petroleum or coal tar pitch, known for its high thermal conductivity and resistance to chemicals. Rayon-Based Carbon Fiber is produced from cellulose, offering good electrical conductivity and cost efficiency. Each type caters to specific industries and applications based on their unique properties.</p></p>
