<p><p>Intraoral scanners are dental devices used to create digital impressions of the teeth and oral tissues. These scanners capture accurate 3D images of the patient's mouth, eliminating the need for traditional, messy dental impressions using dental putty. Intraoral scanners are widely used in dentistry for various applications, including orthodontics, prosthodontics, restorative dentistry, and implantology.</p><p>The market for intraoral scanners has witnessed significant growth in recent years. The increasing demand for efficient and accurate diagnostic tools in dentistry, along with the growing prevalence of dental diseases and disorders, has been driving the market growth. Moreover, the advancements in digital dentistry and the integration of intraoral scanners with computer-aided design and computer-aided manufacturing (CAD/CAM) systems have further boosted market growth.</p><p>The market is also driven by the advantages offered by intraoral scanners over traditional dental impressions. Intraoral scanners provide better patient comfort, reduce chair-time, and offer superior accuracy in capturing dental impressions. Additionally, the growing adoption of digital dentistry and the increasing awareness among dentists and patients about the benefits of intraoral scanners have contributed to market growth.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing the development of technologically advanced scanners with improved scanning capabilities and enhanced software features. These scanners offer faster scanning time, higher accuracy, and seamless integration with CAD/CAM systems. Additionally, manufacturers are focusing on producing lightweight and portable intraoral scanners to improve the ease of use and mobility for dentists.</p><p>Overall, the intraoral scanners market is expected to continue its growth trajectory during the forecast period. The market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 8%, driven by the increasing adoption of digital dentistry, advancements in scanner technology, and the growing demand for accurate and efficient dental diagnostic tools.</p></p>
