<p><p>Forward Collision Warning Systems are safety features that help drivers avoid potential collisions by alerting them of obstacles in their path. These systems can utilize different technologies such as Radar, Laser, and Camera to detect objects ahead of the vehicle. Radar systems use radio waves to measure the distance between the vehicle and obstacles, while Laser systems use pulses of light for the same purpose. Camera systems rely on image recognition software to detect objects and alert the driver accordingly. Each technology has its own advantages and limitations in terms of accuracy and range.</p></p>
