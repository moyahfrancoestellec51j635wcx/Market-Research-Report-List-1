<p><ul><li>Segmented Molds</li><li>Two-Piece Molds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Segmented molds in the tire mold market refer to molds that are divided into multiple sections, allowing for more complex tire designs and tread patterns to be created. These molds are typically used for high-performance or specialty tires. On the other hand, two-piece molds consist of two separate halves that are joined together to create a single tire mold. These molds are more commonly used for producing standard or mass-produced tires. Both types of molds play a crucial role in the tire manufacturing industry, catering to different market demands.</p></p>
