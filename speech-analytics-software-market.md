<p><p>The speech analytics software market comprises two primary types: On-premise and Cloud-based. On-premise speech analytics software is installed and runs on the company's servers and infrastructure, allowing for more control and customization but requiring higher maintenance costs. In contrast, cloud-based speech analytics software is hosted on the provider's servers and accessed through the internet. This type offers greater scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness, as the provider handles maintenance and updates. Both types aim to analyze and extract insights from spoken interactions, improving customer service, sales, and compliance for businesses.</p></p>
