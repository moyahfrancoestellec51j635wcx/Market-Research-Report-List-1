<p><p>Leather protection creams can be classified into two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based leather protection creams are formulated using water as the main solvent, making them eco-friendly and safer for the environment. On the other hand, solvent-based creams are formulated using chemical solvents, providing a stronger and more durable protection for leather goods. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications based on the level of protection and finish desired for the leather item.</p></p>
