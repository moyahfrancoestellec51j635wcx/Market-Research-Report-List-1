<p><p>Semi-Refined Carrageenan (SRC) is a commonly used food ingredient derived from red seaweed. It is categorized into three main types: Kappa Carrageenan, Iota Carrageenan, and Lambda Carrageenan. </p><p>-Kappa Carrageenan is known for its ability to form strong, rigid gels in dairy and meat products.</p><p>-Iota Carrageenan creates soft and elastic gels, often used in dairy desserts and jellies.</p><p>-Lambda Carrageenan does not gel but instead functions as a thickening agent in dairy products and beverages. </p><p>Each type offers unique properties and benefits in various food applications.</p></p>
