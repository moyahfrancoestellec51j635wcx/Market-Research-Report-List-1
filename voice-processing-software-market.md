<p><p>Voice processing software is categorized into two types: On-Premise and Cloud-based market. On-Premise software is installed on the user's local servers, providing complete control and customization but requiring maintenance and infrastructure costs. Cloud-based software, on the other hand, is hosted on third-party servers, providing accessibility from anywhere with an internet connection. This option eliminates maintenance costs and offers scalability but might have limited customization. Both types offer solutions for speech recognition, voice biometrics, transcription, and other voice-related tasks, catering to different user preferences and requirements.</p></p>
