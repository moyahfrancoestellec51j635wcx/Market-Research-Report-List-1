<p><p>Food bulking agents market includes various types such as flavors & enhancers, antioxidants, anticaking agents, emulsifiers, acids, sweeteners, stabilizers, humectants, and others. Anticaking agents prevent clumping in powdered products, emulsifiers help mix ingredients that do not naturally combine, and acids are used for preservation and flavor enhancement. Sweeteners provide sweetness without adding calories, stabilizers maintain texture and consistency, and humectants retain moisture in food products.</p></p>
