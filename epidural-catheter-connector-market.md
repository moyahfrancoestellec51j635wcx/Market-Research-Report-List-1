<p><ul><li>Single Use</li><li>Reuse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epidural catheter connector market consists of two types: Single-use and Reuse. Single-use connectors are intended for one-time use only and are disposed of after each use, ensuring patient safety and preventing infection transmission. On the other hand, Reuse connectors are designed to be sterilized and reused multiple times, offering cost-effectiveness but require meticulous cleaning and maintenance to avoid contamination risks. Both types serve the purpose of connecting epidural catheters securely and efficiently, but they differ in terms of convenience, safety, and cost considerations.</p></p>
