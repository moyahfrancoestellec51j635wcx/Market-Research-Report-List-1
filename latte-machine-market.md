<p><p>The latte machine market can be categorized into four main types: manual, semi-automatic, fully automatic, and super-automatic. Fully automatic machines handle all aspects of brewing with just the push of a button. Super-automatic machines are the most advanced, offering features such as built-in grinders and milk frothers for the ultimate convenience in making lattes.</p></p>
