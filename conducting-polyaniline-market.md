<p><ul><li>Powder Conducting Polyaniline</li><li>Particle Conducting Polyaniline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conducting Polyaniline market comprises of two main types: Powder Conducting Polyaniline and Particle Conducting Polyaniline. Powder Conducting Polyaniline is in the form of fine powder particles, which are easily dispersible and suitable for applications requiring a uniform coating. On the other hand, Particle Conducting Polyaniline consists of larger particles, ideal for applications where a thicker coating is needed or when a slower release of the conducting material is desired. Both types offer unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
