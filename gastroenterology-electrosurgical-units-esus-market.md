<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Vessel Sealing Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gastroenterology Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are used in surgical procedures to cut or coagulate tissue using high-frequency electrical currents. Monopole ESUs use a single electrode for precise cutting, while Bipolar ESUs use two closely spaced electrodes for controlled coagulation. Vessel Sealing Units are specifically designed to seal blood vessels during surgery. Each type of ESU offers unique advantages in gastroenterology procedures, providing surgeons with options for different surgical needs.</p></p>
