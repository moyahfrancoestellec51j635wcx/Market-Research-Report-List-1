<p><p>Petrochemical packaging encompasses two main types: flexible packaging and rigid packaging. Flexible packaging includes items such as bags, pouches, and wraps that can easily conform to the shape of the product being packaged. Rigid packaging, on the other hand, includes containers like bottles, jars, and cans that provide a more stable and protective environment for the product. Both types of packaging play a crucial role in the petrochemical industry by ensuring the safe storage and transportation of various chemical products.</p></p>
