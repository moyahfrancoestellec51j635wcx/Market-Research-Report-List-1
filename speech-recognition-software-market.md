<p><p>Speech recognition software market types include isolated word recognition, keyword spotting, and continuous speech recognition. </p><p>Isolated word recognition refers to systems that can only recognize specific words or phrases, such as simple voice commands. </p><p>Keyword spotting involves recognizing specific keywords within a larger sentence or conversation, enabling users to trigger actions based on those keywords. </p><p>Continuous speech recognition is the most advanced type, capable of understanding and transcribing full sentences and conversations in real-time, providing a more natural and seamless user experience.</p></p>
