Synthetic oils are man-made lubricants with enhanced performance properties, such as higher viscosity index and better oxidation resistance. They are preferred for high-temperature and heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, Mineral oils are derived from natural sources and are suitable for standard operating conditions. They are cost-effective but may require more frequent replacement compared to synthetic oils due to lower thermal stability.</p></p>
