<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Spiral Welded Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ERW Pipes</li><li>LSAW Pipes</li><li>SSAW Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spiral welded pipes are classified into three main types based on the welding process used: ERW pipes, LSAW pipes, and SSAW pipes. ERW pipes are made using electric resistance welding, LSAW pipes are manufactured using longitudinal submerged arc welding, while SSAW pipes are produced through spiral submerged arc welding. Each type of pipe has its own unique characteristics and applications, catering to different industries and structural requirements. The choice of pipe type depends on factors like material, diameter, and operating conditions.</p></p>
