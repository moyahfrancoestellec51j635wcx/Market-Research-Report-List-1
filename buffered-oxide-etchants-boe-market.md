<p><p>Buffered Oxide Etchants (BOE) are used in the semiconductor industry for etching silicon dioxide. Commonly used types of BOE solutions include BOE 6:1 and BOE 7:1, which refer to the concentration ratios of hydrofluoric acid to ammonium fluoride in the solution. BOE 6:1 is commonly used for etching thin oxide layers, while BOE 7:1 is used for thicker oxide layers. Other variations of BOE solutions may exist with different concentration ratios tailored for specific etching requirements in semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
