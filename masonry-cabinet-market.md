<p><p>The masonry cabinet market offers two popular options: quartz and marble. Quartz is a durable and non-porous material composed of real quartz stone mixed with resin, providing a wide range of colors and patterns. It offers easy maintenance, is resistant to scratches and stains, and has become a popular choice for its durability. On the other hand, marble offers a luxurious and elegant look with its natural veins and patterns. However, it requires more delicate care due to its porous nature and is susceptible to stains and etching.</p></p>
