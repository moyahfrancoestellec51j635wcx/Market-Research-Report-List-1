<p><p>Dental laboratories refer to businesses or facilities that fabricate various dental appliances and prosthetics. The market for dental laboratories can be categorized into three types: bridges, crowns, and dentures. Bridges are custom-made dental prosthetics used to replace one or more missing teeth by connecting the artificial teeth to adjacent natural teeth. Crowns are tooth-shaped caps that are placed over damaged or decayed teeth to restore their shape, size, and strength. Dentures, on the other hand, are removable replacements for missing teeth and surrounding tissues. These dental appliances cater to the needs of individuals seeking dental restoration and improved oral functionality.</p></p>
