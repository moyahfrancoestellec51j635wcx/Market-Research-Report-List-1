<p><p>One Time Password (OTP) Display Card market includes two main types: OTP Magnetic Card and OTP Chip Card. OTP Magnetic Cards use a magnetic strip to generate and display one-time passwords for secure authentication. OTP Chip Cards, on the other hand, utilize a microchip embedded in the card to generate and display dynamic passwords. Both types of cards provide an extra layer of security for online transactions and account logins, making them popular choices in today's digital age.</p></p>
