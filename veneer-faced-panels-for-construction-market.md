<p><p>Veneer-faced panels are commonly used in construction for their durability and aesthetic appeal. There are two main types of veneer-faced panels on the market: particleboard faced and MDF faced. Particleboard faced panels are made from wood particles bonded together with adhesive and covered with a thin layer of veneer. MDF faced panels are made from compressed wood fibers and also feature a thin veneer layer. Both types of panels offer a cost-effective and versatile option for construction projects.</p></p>
