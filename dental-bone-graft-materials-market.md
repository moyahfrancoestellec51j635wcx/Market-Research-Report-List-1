<p><p>Dental bone graft materials market types include allograft, xenograft, and synthetic options. Allografts are derived from human donors, xenografts come from animal sources, and synthetic materials are man-made. Allografts are popular due to their compatibility with the patient's own bone, xenografts are widely available and cost-effective, and synthetic materials offer a consistent supply without the need for donor tissues. Each type has its own advantages and considerations, allowing dentists to choose the most suitable option for their patient's specific needs.</p></p>
