<p><ul><li>Character LCD</li><li>Graphic LCD</li><li>Segmented Glass LCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mono LCD displays are divided into three main types: Character LCD, Graphic LCD, and Segmented Glass LCD. Character LCD displays are used for displaying text and numbers in a limited space, typically found in appliances and industrial applications. Graphic LCD displays provide higher resolution and can display images and icons in addition to text, commonly used in electronic devices like calculators and medical equipment. Segmented Glass LCD displays are designed for displaying segmented information, such as digital clocks and instrument panels in vehicles.</p></p>
