<p><p>Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) Systems are security systems designed to prevent theft in retail stores by tagging merchandise with electronic tags and alarms that trigger when exiting the store without proper deactivation. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.9% during the forecast period. Advancements in technology, such as the development of RFID-based EAS systems, are further fueling market growth. Additionally, the rising adoption of EAS systems in various industries beyond retail, such as libraries and museums, is contributing to market expansion.</p><p>The latest trends in the Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) Systems Market include the integration of EAS systems with inventory management software for improved tracking and monitoring of merchandise. The demand for discreet and aesthetically pleasing EAS tags that do not detract from the shopping experience is also driving innovation in the market. Furthermore, the adoption of cloud-based EAS solutions for remote monitoring and management is gaining traction among retailers seeking efficient security solutions.</p></p>
