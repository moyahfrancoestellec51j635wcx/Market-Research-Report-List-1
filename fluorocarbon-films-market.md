<p><ul><li>PTFE</li><li>PVDF</li><li>PEVE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorocarbon films are categorized into three main types: PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene), PVDF (polyvinylidene fluoride), and PEVE (polyethylene vinyl ether). PTFE films are known for their excellent chemical resistance and low friction properties, making them ideal for various industrial applications. PVDF films offer high tensile strength and thermal stability, commonly used in the aerospace and automotive industries. PEVE films are appreciated for their high gas barrier properties and moisture resistance, suitable for food packaging and electronic components.</p></p>
