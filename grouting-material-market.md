<p><p>Grouting materials, such as urethane-based and epoxy-based grouts, are commonly used in construction to fill gaps and spaces between tiles, stones, and other surfaces. Urethane-based grouts provide flexibility and are suitable for areas prone to movement or vibration. They are also resistant to stains and chemicals. In contrast, epoxy-based grouts offer high strength and durability, making them perfect for high-traffic areas. They are resistant to moisture and staining. Both types of grouts provide different benefits and are selected based on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
