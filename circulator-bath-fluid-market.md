<p><p>Circulator bath fluid markets can be divided into two main types: thermal circulating fluid and cold circulating fluid. Thermal circulating fluid is designed to maintain a consistent temperature in laboratory equipment such as circulator baths or chillers. This type of fluid is typically used in applications where precise temperature control is critical. On the other hand, cold circulating fluid is used for cooling purposes, such as in refrigeration systems or to cool down heat-generating equipment. Both types play a crucial role in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and research laboratories.</p></p>
