<p><ul><li>Simplex Active Optical Connectors</li><li>Duplex Active Optical Connectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Optical Connectors refer to high-speed optical connectors that use electrical-to-optical conversion circuits to transmit data over optical fibers. They are classified into two types: Simplex and Duplex. Simplex Active Optical Connectors have a single fiber connection, ideal for single-directional communication. Duplex Active Optical Connectors consist of two fibers within a single connector, enabling bi-directional communication. These connectors are widely used in applications such as data centers, telecommunications, and high-speed computing, providing reliable and efficient optical connections for various industries.</p></p>
