<p><strong>The Impregnation Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Impregnation resins are divided into two main types - Type I and Type II. Type I resins are typically thermosetting resins that are cured at high temperatures, offering excellent heat resistance and electrical insulation properties. On the other hand, Type II resins are thermoplastic resins that can be reheated and reformed, making them more flexible and easier to process. Both types of resins are widely used in impregnation processes to enhance the performance and durability of various materials.</p></p>
