<p><strong>The Optical Fibre Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber</li><li>Multimode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical fibre cable market is divided into two main types: Single Mode Fiber and Multimode Fiber. Single Mode Fiber is designed for long-distance communication and is used in applications that require a higher bandwidth. Multimode Fiber, on the other hand, is used for shorter distances and is cost-effective for local area networks. Single Mode Fiber has a smaller core diameter compared to Multimode Fiber, allowing only one mode of light to transmit through it, whereas Multimode Fiber allows multiple modes of light to transmit simultaneously.</p></p>
