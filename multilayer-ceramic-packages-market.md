<p><p>Multilayer Ceramic Packages Market includes various types such as Ceramic-Metal Sealing (CERTM), Glass-Metal Sealing (GTMS), Passivation Glass, Transponder Glass, and Reed Glass. CERTM packages use ceramic and metal to create a hermetic seal, while GTMS packages use glass and metal for sealing. Passivation Glass packages protect electronic components from environmental factors. Transponder Glass packages are used in RFID technology, and Reed Glass packages are utilized in sensors and switches. Each type offers unique benefits for different applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
