<p><p>Silane Modified Polymers (SMP) adhesives and sealants are divided into two main market types: One Component and Two Component. One Component SMP adhesives and sealants come ready to use and require no mixing, making them convenient for quick application. On the other hand, Two Component SMP products involve mixing separate components before application, allowing for greater control over curing time and adhesive strength. Both types offer excellent adhesion, durability, and weather resistance for various construction and industrial applications.</p></p>
