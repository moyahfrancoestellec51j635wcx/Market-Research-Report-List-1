<p><p>Fire Alarm Control Panel (FACP) market consists of two main types: Conventional and Addressable systems. Conventional systems use zones to divide a building into different areas for monitoring, whereas Addressable systems enable each device to have a unique address, allowing for specific identification of the location of a fire or fault. Conventional systems are more cost-effective for smaller buildings, while Addressable systems offer advanced features and precise pinpointing of incidents, making them suitable for larger and complex structures.</p></p>
