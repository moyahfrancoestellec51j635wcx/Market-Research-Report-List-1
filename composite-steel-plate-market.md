<p><strong>The Composite Steel Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Explosion Bonded</li><li>Roll Bonded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Composite steel plate market includes explosion bonded and roll bonded types. Explosion bonded plates are created by placing two metal plates next to each other and using explosives to bond them together. This process creates a strong bond that is resistant to corrosion and wear. Roll bonded plates are made by rolling two plates together under high pressure and temperature, creating a metallurgical bond between them. Both types of composite steel plates are used in various industries for their high strength and durability.</p></p>
