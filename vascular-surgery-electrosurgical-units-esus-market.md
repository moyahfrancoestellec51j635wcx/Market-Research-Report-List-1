<p><p>Vascular Surgery Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are devices used in surgical procedures that involve blood vessels. The market for ESUs consists of various types, including Monopole Electrosurgical Units, Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, and Vessel Sealing Units. Monopole units deliver electrical energy through a single electrode, while Bipolar units use two electrodes to safely control bleeding. Vessel Sealing units are designed specifically to seal blood vessels during surgery, reducing the risk of complications. These different types cater to the specific needs of vascular surgeons and offer varying benefits in terms of precision and efficiency.</p></p>
