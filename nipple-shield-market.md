<p><p>Regular nipple shields are designed for mothers experiencing soreness or pain while breastfeeding. Contact nipple shields are specifically designed for mothers with flat or inverted nipples, helping the baby latch onto the breast more easily. Both types of nipple shields are available in various sizes and materials to accommodate individual needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
