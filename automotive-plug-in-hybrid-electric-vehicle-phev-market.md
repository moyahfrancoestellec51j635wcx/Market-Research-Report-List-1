<p><strong>The Automotive Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Plug-in</li><li>Parallel or Hybrid Plug-in</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) are available in three market types: Series Plug-in, Parallel, and Hybrid Plug-in. In a Series Plug-in, the internal combustion engine is used only to generate electricity for the electric motor. In a Parallel PHEV, both the electric motor and the internal combustion engine can power the wheels simultaneously. Hybrid Plug-ins combine both Series and Parallel functionalities, allowing for different power sources to work together based on driving conditions, optimizing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
