<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Elbow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polypropylene quick couplings come in two main types: straight and elbow. Straight couplings are designed for applications where a straight connection is needed, providing a direct and efficient flow of fluids. On the other hand, elbow couplings are used in situations where space is limited or where a connection needs to be made at an angle. Elbow couplings allow for flexibility in positioning and maneuvering hoses or pipes, making them ideal for various industrial and agricultural applications. Both types offer quick and easy connections for efficient fluid transfer.</p></p>
