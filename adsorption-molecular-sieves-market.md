<p><p>Adsorption molecular sieves can be categorized into two main types: A Type and X Type. A Type molecular sieves have a smaller pore size which makes them suitable for drying and purifying gases and liquids. They are commonly used in air separation, natural gas dehydration, and refrigeration systems. On the other hand, X Type molecular sieves have a larger pore size, making them ideal for separating polar and non-polar molecules. They are commonly used in the petrochemical industry for separating hydrocarbons and removing impurities from liquids.</p></p>
