<p><strong>The Melamine-faced Panels for Furniture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MF-C</li><li>MF-MDF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Melamine-faced Panels for Furniture Market includes two main types: MF-C (Melamine-faced chipboard) and MF-MDF (Melamine-faced medium-density fiberboard). </p><p>MF-C panels are made from compressed wood chips covered with a melamine resin coating, making them durable and cost-effective for furniture production. On the other hand, MF-MDF panels are made from wood fibers bonded together with resin and then coated with melamine. They offer a smoother surface and are preferred for higher-end furniture designs. Both types are popular choices in the furniture industry for their versatility and durability.</p></p>
