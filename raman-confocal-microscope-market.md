<p><strong>The Raman Confocal Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>True Confocal</li><li>Pseudo-Confocal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Raman Confocal Microscope market includes True Confocal and Pseudo-Confocal types. True confocal microscopes utilize a pinhole to reject out-of-focus light, resulting in high resolution and contrast images. Pseudo-confocal microscopes, on the other hand, do not have a pinhole and rely on software to create a similar effect. While true confocal microscopes are more expensive and provide superior image quality, pseudo-confocal microscopes are more cost-effective and easier to use, making them suitable for routine laboratory work.</p></p>
