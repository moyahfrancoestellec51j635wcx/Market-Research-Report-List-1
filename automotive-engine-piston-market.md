<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Piston Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trunk pistons</li><li>Crosshead pistons</li><li>Slipper pistons</li><li>Deflector pistons</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive engine piston market types include trunk pistons, which have a simple design and are commonly used in small engines. Crosshead pistons have a unique shape with a connecting rod that prevents the piston from tilting. Slipper pistons have a smaller skirt area for reduced friction. Deflector pistons feature a raised ridge on top to deflect the incoming air and fuel mixture towards the spark plug. Each type of piston is designed to meet specific performance and efficiency requirements in different types of engines.</p></p>
