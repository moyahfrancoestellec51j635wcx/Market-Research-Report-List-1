<p><p>Chemical degreasers are used to remove grease and oil from surfaces in industrial settings. There are three main types of chemical degreasers in the market - alkaline degreasers, emulsion degreasers, and solvent degreasers. Alkaline degreasers work by breaking down grease with the help of alkaline chemicals. Emulsion degreasers use a combination of water and oil to remove grease effectively. Solvent degreasers utilize solvents to dissolve grease and oil. Each type of degreaser has its own unique benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
