<p><p>Traffic sign reflective sheeting market consists of two main types: Glass Bead Type and Micro Prismatic Type. Glass Bead Type sheeting uses tiny glass beads that reflect light, making the sign visible at night. Micro Prismatic Type sheeting is made up of small prisms that reflect and refract light, providing high visibility in both day and night conditions. Both types are essential for ensuring road safety by improving visibility and legibility of traffic signs, ultimately aiding in preventing accidents.</p></p>
