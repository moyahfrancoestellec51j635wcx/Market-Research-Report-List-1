<p><strong>The Flame Retardant Epoxy Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Type Epoxy Resin</li><li>Additive Type Epoxy Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flame retardant epoxy resin market can be categorized into two main types: reactive type and additive type. Reactive type epoxy resin involves incorporating flame retardant elements into the resin during the manufacturing process, ensuring durable and long-lasting flame retardancy. On the other hand, additive type epoxy resin consists of adding flame retardant agents to the resin after it has been manufactured, providing a cost-effective solution for achieving flame retardancy. Both types play crucial roles in meeting the high demand for fire-resistant materials in various industries.</p></p>
