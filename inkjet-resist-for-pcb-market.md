<p><strong>The Inkjet Resist for PCB Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Curable Inkjet Resists</li><li>Thermal Curable Inkjet Resists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inkjet resist for PCB market includes two main types: UV curable inkjet resists and thermal curable inkjet resists. UV curable inkjet resists use ultraviolet light to harden the resist material, providing quick and precise curing. Thermal curable inkjet resists, on the other hand, require heat to cure the resist material. Both types offer high resolution printing capabilities for creating intricate patterns on printed circuit boards, making them essential for the manufacturing of electronic devices.</p></p>
