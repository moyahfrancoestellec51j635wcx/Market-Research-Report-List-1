<p><p>Self-inflating tissue expanders are used in reconstructive surgery for tissue expansion. There are three main types of self-inflating tissue expanders: sphere-shaped, hemisphere-shaped, and pins-shaped. Sphere-shaped tissue expanders are round and ideal for larger areas of tissue expansion. Hemisphere-shaped tissue expanders are half-rounded and suitable for smaller areas. Pins-shaped tissue expanders have a needle-like design, allowing for precise placement in tight spaces. Each type is designed to meet specific needs in tissue expansion procedures.</p></p>
