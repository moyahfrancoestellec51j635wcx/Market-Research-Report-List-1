<p><ul><li>Schottky diodes</li><li>Standard diodes or general purpose diodes</li><li>Fast recovery diodes</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are different types of power diodes in the market, including Schottky diodes, standard diodes (or general-purpose diodes), fast recovery diodes, and others. Schottky diodes have a lower forward voltage drop and faster switching speed, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. Standard diodes are commonly used in general-purpose applications like rectification. Fast recovery diodes have a reduced recovery time, minimizing power loss in switching applications. The "others" category includes specialized diodes like Zener diodes, varactor diodes, and avalanche diodes.</p></p>
