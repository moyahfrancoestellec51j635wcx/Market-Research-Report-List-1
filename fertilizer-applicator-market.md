<p><strong>The Fertilizer Applicator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mounted Fertilizer Applicator</li><li>Trailed Fertilizer Applicator</li><li>Self-Propelled Fertilizer Applicator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mounted fertilizer applicators are devices that are attached to tractors, where the fertilizer is directly applied to the ground as the tractor moves. Trailed fertilizer applicators are connected to tractors through a hitch and are dragged behind the tractor to distribute fertilizer. Self-propelled fertilizer applicators are standalone machines that can move independently through fields to apply fertilizer. Each type of applicator has its own advantages and is used based on the specific needs and requirements of the farmer.</p></p>
