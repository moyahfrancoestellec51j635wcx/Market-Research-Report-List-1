<p><p>TPE conveyor belts are classified into four main types: TPES, TPU, TPO, and TPEE. TPES belts are designed for high-temperature applications, offering excellent resistance to heat and wear. TPU belts are known for their flexibility and durability, making them suitable for a wide range of industries. TPO belts feature a balance of properties including strength and chemical resistance. TPEE belts are an eco-friendly option, known for their recyclability and reduced environmental impact. Each type has specific characteristics to meet different market needs.</p></p>
