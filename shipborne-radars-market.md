<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Shipborne Radars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Electronically Scanned Array (PESA) Radar</li><li>Active Electronically Scanned Array (AESA) Radar</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shipborne radars are used for detecting and tracking targets at sea. Passive Electronically Scanned Array (PESA) radars use fixed antennas to electronically steer the radar beam. Active Electronically Scanned Array (AESA) radars use multiple small transmit/receive modules to electronically steer the radar beam. Other types of shipborne radars include Frequency-Modulated Continuous Wave (FMCW) radars, Doppler radars, and pulse radars. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of target detection, tracking, and overall performance in maritime environments.</p></p>
