<p><p>Brake chambers are an important component in braking systems, with two main types being piston type and diaphragm type. In a piston type brake chamber, the braking force is generated by the movement of a piston within a cylinder. On the other hand, in a diaphragm type brake chamber, the braking force is generated by the deformation of a flexible diaphragm. Each type has its advantages and is used in various applications based on factors such as space constraints and braking force requirements.</p></p>
