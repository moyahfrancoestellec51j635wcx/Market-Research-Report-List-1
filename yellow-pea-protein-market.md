Pea Protein Isolates have higher protein content (85-90%) and lower carbohydrate content compared to concentrates. Textured Pea Protein is a meat substitute made from pea protein. Hydrolyzed Pea Protein is broken down into smaller protein fragments for easier digestion and absorption. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and dietary needs.</p></p>
