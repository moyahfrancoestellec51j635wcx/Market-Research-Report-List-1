<p><p>The vehicle elevator market is comprised of two main types: hydraulic elevators and electric elevators. Hydraulic elevators operate by using fluid to lift the elevator car, making them ideal for buildings with fewer floors. On the other hand, electric elevators use a system of gears and pulleys powered by electricity to move the car. Electric elevators are suitable for taller buildings and are known for their speed and efficiency. Both types of elevators offer reliable transportation for vehicles within buildings.</p></p>
