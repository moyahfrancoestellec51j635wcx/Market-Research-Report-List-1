<p><p>Elastomeric rubber foam insulation materials are classified into different types based on the type of rubber used in their composition. The major types include NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) based, EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) based, Chloroprene based, and others. Each type offers unique properties and benefits, such as resistance to oil and chemicals (NBR), UV resistance and weatherability (EPDM), and flexibility and ozone resistance (Chloroprene). This variety in market types allows consumers to choose the most suitable insulation material for their specific needs.</p></p>
