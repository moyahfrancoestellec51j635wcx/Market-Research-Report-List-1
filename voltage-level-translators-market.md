<p><p>Voltage level translators are devices that enable communication between components operating at different voltage levels. Dual supply level translators facilitate bidirectional voltage translation between two voltage domains, while open drain devices are designed for applications where the output is connected to an external pull-up resistor. Both types of voltage level translators are essential in electronics and are used in a wide range of applications to ensure compatibility and proper communication between different voltage domains.</p></p>
