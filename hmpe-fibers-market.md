<p><p>HMPE fibers market can be categorized into two types: dry process and wet process. In the dry process, the fibers are produced using a solvent-free spinning method, resulting in high strength and low elongation. On the other hand, the wet process involves spinning the fibers in a solvent bath, allowing for better control over the properties of the fibers. Both processes have their own advantages and are used in various industries such as automotive, marine, and aerospace for their unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
