<p><p>Direct organic paper dyes are categorized into two main types: powder and liquid. Powder dyes are finely ground particles that are mixed with water or another solvent to create a dye solution. Liquid dyes, on the other hand, are already in liquid form and can be directly applied to the paper surface. Both types of dyes are derived from natural sources and are considered environmentally friendly alternatives to synthetic dyes. These dyes are widely used in the paper industry for coloring various paper products.</p></p>
