<p><p>The 3D Cameras Market includes various types such as Stereo Vision, Time of Flight, and Structured Light cameras. Stereo Vision cameras use two lenses to perceive depth information and create a 3D image. Time of Flight cameras measure the time it takes for light to bounce back from objects to calculate depth. Structured Light cameras project a pattern of light onto objects and analyze the distortion to determine depth. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in different applications within the market.</p></p>
