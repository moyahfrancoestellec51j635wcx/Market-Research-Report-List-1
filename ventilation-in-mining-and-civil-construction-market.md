<p><p>Ventilation in mining and civil construction refers to the process of introducing fresh air into enclosed spaces, such as mines and construction sites, and removing stale or contaminated air. It helps to prevent the buildup of hazardous gases, such as methane and carbon monoxide, which can be highly dangerous in confined spaces.
