<p><strong>The Polysilicon Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P Type</li><li>N Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polysilicon wafers are classified into P type and N type markets based on the type of dopants used in the manufacturing process. P type polysilicon wafers are doped with boron, creating a positive charge carrier, while N type polysilicon wafers are doped with phosphorus, creating a negative charge carrier. Both types of polysilicon wafers have different electrical properties and are utilized in various applications within the semiconductor industry. The choice of P type or N type depends on the specific requirements of the electronic devices being developed.</p></p>
