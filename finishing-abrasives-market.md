<p><strong>The Finishing Abrasives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bonded</li><li>Coated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Finishing abrasives are classified into two main types: bonded and coated. Bonded abrasives consist of abrasive grains held together by a bonding material, such as resin or ceramic. These abrasives are typically used for precision grinding applications. In contrast, coated abrasives have abrasive grains coated onto a backing material, such as paper or cloth. Coated abrasives are commonly used for sanding and finishing applications. Both types of abrasives play a crucial role in smoothing and polishing surfaces in various industries.</p></p>
