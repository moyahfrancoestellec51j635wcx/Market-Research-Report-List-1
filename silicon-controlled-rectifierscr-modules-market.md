<p><p>SCR modules are electronic devices used for power control applications. Unidirectional SCR modules only conduct current in one direction, making them suitable for applications where power flow is unidirectional. On the other hand, bidirectional SCR modules can control current flow in both directions, making them ideal for applications where power flow needs to be reversible. Both types of SCR modules are widely used in various industries for efficient power control and regulation.</p></p>
