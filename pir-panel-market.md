<p><p>The PIR panel market is categorized into three main types based on thickness: below 51mm, 51mm-100mm, and above 100mm. Panels below 51mm are commonly used for interior applications such as insulation in walls and ceilings. Panels with a thickness of 51mm-100mm are often used for roofing and exterior walls. Panels above 100mm are typically used for industrial applications where high levels of insulation are required, such as cold storage facilities or warehouses. Each thickness category caters to specific insulation needs in various construction projects.</p></p>
