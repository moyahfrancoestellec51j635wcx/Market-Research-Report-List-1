<p><p>Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) membranes are commonly used in construction for waterproofing purposes. There are two main types of PVC membranes: plasticized PVC membranes and unplasticized PVC membranes. Plasticized PVC membranes are flexible and easy to work with, making them suitable for applications that require bending and stretching. Unplasticized PVC membranes, on the other hand, are rigid and provide better resistance to chemicals and UV rays. Both types of PVC membranes are cost-effective and have a long service life, making them popular choices in the construction industry.</p></p>
