<p><p>Silicone diffusion pump fluids are categorized into two main types based on their viscosity: low viscosity silicone oil and high viscosity silicone oil. Low viscosity silicone oil is a lighter fluid that flows easily, making it suitable for processes requiring fast pumping speeds and high vacuum levels. High viscosity silicone oil, on the other hand, is thicker and more resistant to thermal degradation, making it ideal for applications where high temperatures are involved. Both types of silicone oils play crucial roles in maintaining the efficiency and effectiveness of diffusion pumps in various industries.</p></p>
