<p><p>Marble facade cladding can be classified into two main types: Engineered and Natural. Engineered marble facade cladding is manufactured using a combination of marble dust, resins, and other materials to create a durable and versatile product. On the other hand, Natural marble facade cladding is made from solid marble stone, offering a luxurious and authentic look. Both types cater to different market segments, with Engineered marble cladding often preferred for its cost-effectiveness and versatility, while Natural marble cladding is sought after for its beauty and natural characteristics.</p></p>
