<p><p>Commercial deodorizers come in three main types based on their mechanism of action. Physical type deodorizers work by absorbing odors and trapping them, reducing their presence in the air. Chemical type deodorizers contain ingredients that chemically neutralize odors, effectively eliminating them. Microbial type deodorizers use bacteria or enzymes to break down organic matter causing odors. Each type of deodorizer offers a unique solution for combating unwanted smells in commercial spaces, catering to different preferences and needs.</p></p>
