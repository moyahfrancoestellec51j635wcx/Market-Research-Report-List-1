<p><strong>The Wood Based Flooring Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Particleboard (PB)</li><li>Medium density fibreboard (MDF)</li><li>High density fibreboard (HDF)</li><li>Oriented strand board (OSB)</li><li>Plywood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wood based flooring panel market types consist of Particleboard (PB), Medium density fibreboard (MDF), High density fibreboard (HDF), Oriented strand board (OSB), and Plywood. Particleboard is made from wood chips bonded together with resin. MDF is produced from dried wood fibers bonded with wax and resin under high pressure. HDF is similar to MDF but denser and stronger. OSB is made from wood strands arranged in oriented layers. Plywood is made from thin layers of wood veneer glued together with grains running perpendicular to each other.</p></p>
