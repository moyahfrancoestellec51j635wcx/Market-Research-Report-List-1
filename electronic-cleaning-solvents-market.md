<p><p>Electronic cleaning solvents market is categorized into several types such as Formulated Hydrocarbon Solvents, Glycols & Glycol Ethers, Fluorinated Solvents, Brominated Solvents, and Light Petroleum Distillates. Formulated Hydrocarbon Solvents are typically used for removing oils, greases, and flux residues. Glycols & Glycol Ethers are effective in cleaning polar contaminants. Fluorinated Solvents are used for precision cleaning as they evaporate quickly without leaving any residues. Brominated Solvents are mainly used for heavy-duty cleaning applications. Light Petroleum Distillates are versatile solvents suitable for various cleaning needs in the electronics industry.</p></p>

<p><p>Electronic cleaning solvents are used in various processes such as vapor phase degreaser, vacuum cleaning, mixed co-solvent, separated co-solvent, and semi-aqueous processes. The vapor phase degreaser process involves using solvents in a closed chamber to remove contaminants. Vacuum cleaning process utilizes solvents to clean electronic components using a vacuum. Mixed co-solvent process combines different solvents for effective cleaning. Separated co-solvent process involves separating solvents after cleaning. Semi-aqueous process uses a combination of water and solvents for cleaning electronic parts.</p></p>
