<p><strong>The Sol-Gel Processing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotational Coating</li><li>Immersion Coating</li><li>Roll Coating</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sol-Gel processing market types include rotational coating, immersion coating, roll coating, and others. Rotational coating involves spinning the substrate while applying the sol-gel solution to create a uniform film. Immersion coating immerses the substrate into the sol-gel solution to coat the entire surface. Roll coating uses a roller to apply the sol-gel solution onto the substrate. Other methods may include spray coating, dip coating, and spin coating, each offering unique advantages for different applications in the sol-gel processing industry.</p></p>
