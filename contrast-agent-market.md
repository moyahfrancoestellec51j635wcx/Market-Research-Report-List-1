<p><p>The contrast agent market consists of three main types: Gadolinium-Based Contrast Media, Barium-Based Contrast Media, and Iodinated Contrast Media. Gadolinium-Based Contrast Media is used in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) to enhance visualization of certain body parts. Barium-Based Contrast Media is utilized in X-ray exams, particularly for examining the gastrointestinal tract. Iodinated Contrast Media is employed in various diagnostic imaging procedures, such as computed tomography (CT) scans, to improve visibility of blood vessels and certain organs. These contrast agents assist in providing clearer and more accurate images for medical diagnoses.</p></p>
