<p><p>Under Cabinet Type Hoods are kitchen ventilation systems that are installed under kitchen cabinets. There are two main types of hoods available in the market: Axial Flow Hood and Centrifugal Hood. Axial Flow Hood directs air flow in a straight line, providing effective ventilation for light cooking. Centrifugal Hood uses a fan to create centrifugal force, which helps in capturing smoke and grease particles more efficiently, making it suitable for heavy-duty cooking. Both types cater to different cooking needs and preferences.</p></p>
