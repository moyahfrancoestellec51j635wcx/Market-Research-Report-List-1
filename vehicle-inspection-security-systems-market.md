<p><p>Vehicle Inspection Security Systems come in three main types: Permanent, Fixed, and Portable. Permanent systems are embedded in the road, providing continuous monitoring and inspection. Fixed systems are attached to the road's surface at specific locations, offering targeted security checks. Portable systems are mobile units that can be deployed to different locations as needed, allowing for flexibility in inspection processes. Each type of system has its own advantages and can be used in various scenarios to enhance vehicle security measures.</p></p>
