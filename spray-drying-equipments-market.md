<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Two-stage</li><li>Multi-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray drying equipments market can be categorized into single stage, two stage, and multi-stage types. Single stage spray drying equipments involve a single step process where liquid is sprayed into a chamber, dried, and collected. Two-stage spray drying equipments have separate drying and cooling chambers, allowing for better control over temperature and moisture content. Multi-stage spray drying equipments have multiple chambers for drying, cooling, and collecting the dried product, making them suitable for complex drying processes. Each type offers specific advantages depending on the requirements of the application.</p></p>
