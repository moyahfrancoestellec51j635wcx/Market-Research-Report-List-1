<p><p>Polyethylene Low Density (LDPE) is a type of thermoplastic made from ethylene. LDPE is produced using two main processes: Autoclave Process and Tubular Process. The Autoclave Process involves high pressure and high temperature in a reactor, resulting in a more uniform product. The Tubular Process uses a series of connected tubes to produce LDPE in a continuous manner. Both processes are widely used in the market for various applications due to their efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
