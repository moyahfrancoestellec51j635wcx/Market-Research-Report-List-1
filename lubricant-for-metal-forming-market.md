<p><p>Lubricants for metal forming come in various types to meet specific requirements. Tube drawing lubricants are designed for the process of pulling a metal tube through a die to decrease friction and prevent wrinkling. Hot forming lubricants are used for metalworking processes that involve high temperatures, such as forging or extrusion, to reduce wear and tear on tools and machinery. Both types of lubricants are essential for enhancing the efficiency and quality of metal forming operations.</p></p>
