<p><p>Safety interlock switches are devices used to prevent unauthorized access to potentially hazardous machinery or equipment. Noncontact safety interlock switches use sensors to detect the presence of an object without physical contact, providing a reliable and durable solution for safety applications. On the other hand, mechanical safety interlock switches require physical interaction to engage or disengage the switch, offering a more traditional but effective method of ensuring safety. Both types of safety interlock switches play a crucial role in maintaining a safe working environment in industrial settings.</p></p>
