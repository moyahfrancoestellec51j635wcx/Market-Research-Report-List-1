<p><p>Polycarbonate compound market can be divided into two main types: clear polycarbonate and unclear polycarbonate. Clear polycarbonate is a transparent material that allows for excellent light transmission, making it suitable for applications where visibility is important. On the other hand, unclear polycarbonate has a less transparent appearance, offering a more opaque finish. Both types of polycarbonate compounds have their own set of advantages and are widely used in various industries for their durability, impact resistance, and versatility.</p></p>
