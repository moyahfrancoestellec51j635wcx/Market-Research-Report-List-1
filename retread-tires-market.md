<p><p>Retread tires market can be categorized into two main types based on the process used: Pre Cure Process and Mold Cure Process. In the Pre Cure Process, a new tread is applied to the tire casing prior to vulcanization, whereas in the Mold Cure Process the new tread is applied and cured in a mold. Each process has its own advantages and disadvantages, and both types cater to different customer needs and preferences within the retread tire market.</p></p>
