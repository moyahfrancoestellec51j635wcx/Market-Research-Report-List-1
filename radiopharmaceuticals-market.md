<p><ul><li>Diagnostic Radioisotopes</li><li>Therapeutic Radioisotopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiopharmaceuticals are classified into two main types: Diagnostic Radioisotopes and Therapeutic Radioisotopes. Diagnostic radioisotopes are used in medical imaging to diagnose and visualize diseases or conditions within the body. They emit gamma rays that can be detected by imaging devices. Therapeutic radioisotopes, on the other hand, are used in radiation therapy to treat various diseases such as cancer. They emit radiation that targets and destroys cancer cells. Both types play a crucial role in modern medicine for diagnosis and treatment purposes.</p></p>
