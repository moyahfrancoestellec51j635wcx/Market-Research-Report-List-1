<p><p>The PET/MRI System market offers three main types of systems: Fully Integrated, Separate Devices, and Pulse Oximetry Screening. Fully Integrated systems combine PET and MRI capabilities into one machine, allowing for simultaneous imaging. Separate Devices consist of independent PET and MRI machines used in conjunction for imaging. Pulse Oximetry Screening systems include additional monitoring for measuring oxygen levels in patients during imaging procedures. Each type of system offers unique benefits and applications within the medical imaging field.</p></p>
