<p><p>Field mount temperature transmitters come in various types such as thermistors, bimetallic temperature transmitters, resistive temperature detectors, fiber optic temperature transmitters, and thermocouples. Thermistors use the resistance of a semiconductor material to measure temperature, while bimetallic temperature transmitters use two different metals to create a temperature-sensitive switch. Resistive temperature detectors measure temperature by changes in resistance, fiber optic temperature transmitters use light to measure temperature, and thermocouples work by measuring the voltage produced by two different metals at different temperatures.</p></p>
