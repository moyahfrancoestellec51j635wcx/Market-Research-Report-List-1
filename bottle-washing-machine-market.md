<p><p>Bottle washing machines can be classified into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machines are fully self-operating, requiring minimal human involvement and able to wash a large volume of bottles efficiently. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some human intervention, such as loading and unloading the bottles, but still offer automated cleaning functions. Both types cater to different market needs, with automatic machines being suitable for high-capacity production lines and semi-automatic machines being more cost-effective for smaller scale operations.</p></p>
