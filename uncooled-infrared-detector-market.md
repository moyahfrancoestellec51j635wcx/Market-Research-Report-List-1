Bolometers use changes in electrical resistance to detect infrared radiation, Pyroelectric Detectors generate a voltage in response to temperature changes, Thermopile Detectors use temperature differences to generate a voltage, Diode Detectors operate based on changes in voltage or current, Thermal Capacitance Detectors measure changes in heat capacitance, and other detectors utilize different technologies for infrared detection.</p></p>
